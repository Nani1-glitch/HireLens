# Hirelens - Complete Feature List for Marketing

## Core Job Analysis Features

### 1. AI-Powered Job Quality Analysis
Analyze any job posting to get instant quality scores for salary, location, cost of living, and red flags. Make informed decisions before applying with our comprehensive job evaluation system.

### 2. Resume ATS Check
Get your resume's ATS compatibility score and see exactly which keywords match and which are missing. Improve your chances of passing automated screening systems with actionable insights.

### 3. AI Resume Optimizer Pro
Transform your resume bullets into ATS-friendly, impactful statements that boost your match score. Get AI-powered rewrites with explanations for each improvement.

### 4. AI Cover Letter Generator
Generate personalized, professional cover letters tailored to specific job postings in seconds. Highlight your relevant experience and show why you're the perfect fit.

### 5. Salary Negotiation Advisor
Analyze job offers with market data, cost of living adjustments, and get negotiation scripts. Know your worth and negotiate confidently with AI-powered salary insights.

### 6. Multi-Resume Comparison Tool
Upload multiple resume versions and see which one best matches a job description. Compare strengths, weaknesses, and match scores side-by-side to choose your strongest resume.

## Career Development Features

### 7. Skill Gap Analysis Tool
Identify the skills you're missing for your dream job and get personalized learning recommendations. Bridge the gap between where you are and where you want to be with actionable career development paths.

### 8. Resume Score History & Progress Tracking
Track your resume improvements over time with visual timelines and statistics. See your progress, celebrate milestones, and watch your scores improve with each optimization.

### 9. Job Application Tracker Dashboard
Organize all your job applications in one place with status tracking, interview scheduling, and follow-up reminders. Never lose track of an application again.

## Job Discovery Features

### 10. Resume to Job Matcher
Upload your resume and instantly find matching job opportunities from across the web. Get personalized job recommendations ranked by how well they match your skills and experience.

### 11. Job Alert System with AI Filtering
Receive notifications about high-quality job postings that match your preferences. Avoid spam with AI-powered filtering that only shows you relevant, quality opportunities.

### 12. Salary Spread Analysis
Get comprehensive salary distribution data for any job title and location. See percentiles, market averages, and salary ranges to negotiate from a position of knowledge.

## Engagement & Social Features

### 13. Achievement Badges & Gamification
Earn badges for milestones like high resume scores, applications tracked, and skill improvements. Turn job searching into an engaging journey with gamified achievements.

### 14. LinkedIn Profile Sharing
Share your analysis results and achievements on LinkedIn with one click. Showcase your progress and help others discover Hirelens through viral social sharing.

### 15. Anonymous Leaderboard
Compare your resume quality anonymously with other users. See where you rank and get motivated to improve with friendly competition.

### 16. Real-Time Activity Feed
See what's happening on the platform with anonymous user activities. Get inspired by others' progress and feel part of an active community.

## Company Interaction Features

### 17. Company-Applicant Interaction Tracking
See which jobs are most active based on applicant interactions. Jobs with more engagement are more likely to be actively hiring.

### 18. Company Reach Outs
Get notified when companies reach out based on your profile match. High match scores trigger automatic company interest notifications.
